About:

Welcome to StoryMap, an interactive mapping platform that empowers users to geo-spatially visualize any story!

Map your favorite novel, film, or personal experience through StoryMap. Upload custom maps, create custom tags, and explore other channels to check out more user-made story maps!

StoryMap is designed to be a collaborative and educational environment where people connect, engage, and share stories.
